# Angle of Shear - Angle of Twist Relation

Figure

$\theta$: Angle of Twist | $\phi$: Angle of Shear | $*r*$: Radius

$AA' =  r\theta$ #1

$AA' = l\phi$ #2

From #1 and #2,

$\Rightarrow r\theta = l\phi$

Hence:
$$\theta = \frac{l\phi}{r}$$
$$\phi = \frac{r\theta}{l}$$
